CHAPTER 10
THE [[ X X X ] APPELLATE TRIBUNAL]

60. Limitation

The provisions of the Limitation Act, 1963, shall, as far as may be, apply to an
appeal made to the [Appellate Tribunal].

OBJECTS AND REASONS
Clause 60.This clause provides for period of limitation for admission of appeals
from the aggrieved persons to the Cyber Appellate Tribunal.
