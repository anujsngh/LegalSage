CHAPTER 11
OFFENCES

72A. Punishment for disclosure of information in breach of lawful contract

Save as otherwise provided in this Act or any other law for the time being in force,
any person including an intermediary who, while providing services under the terms of
lawful contract, has secured access to any material containing personal information about
another person, with the intent to cause or knowing that he is likely to cause wrongful
loss or wrongful gain discloses, without the consent of the person concerned, or in breach
of a lawful contract, such material to any other person, shall be punished with
imprisonment for a term which may extend to three years, or with fine which may extend
to five lakh rupees, or with both.
