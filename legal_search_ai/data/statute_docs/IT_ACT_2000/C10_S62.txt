CHAPTER 10
THE [[ X X X ] APPELLATE TRIBUNAL]

62. Appeal to High Court

Any person aggrieved by any decision or order of the a[Appellate Tribunal] may file
an appeal to the High Court within sixty days from the date of communication of the
decision or order of the a[Appellate Tribunal] to him on any question of fact or law
arising out of such order :
Provided that the High Court may, if it is satisfied that the appellant was prevented
by sufficient cause from filing the appeal within the said period, allow it to be filed within
a further period not exceeding sixty days.

OBJECTS AND REASONS
Clause 62.This clause provides for an appeal to the High Court by an aggrieved
person from the decision of the Cyber Appellate Tribunal.
