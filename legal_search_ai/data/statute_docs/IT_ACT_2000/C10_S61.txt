CHAPTER 10
THE [[ X X X ] APPELLATE TRIBUNAL]

61. Civil Court not to have jurisdiction

No court shall have jurisdiction to entertain any suit or proceeding in respect of any
matter which an adjudicating officer appointed under this Act or the [Appellate Tribunal]
constituted under this Act is empowered by or under this Act to determine and no
injunction shall be granted by any court or other authority in respect of any action taken
or to be taken in pursuance of any power conferred by or under this Act.

OBJECTS AND REASONS
Clause 61. This clause provides that no court shall have jurisdiction to entertain any
suit or proceeding in respect of any matter which an adjudicating officer has jurisdiction
to determine.
