CHAPTER 13
MISCELLANEOUS

81. Act to have overriding effect

The provisions of this Act shall have effect notwithstanding anything inconsistent
therewith contained in any other law for the time being in force.
[Provided that nothing contained in this Act shall restrict any person from
exercising any right conferred under the Copyright Act, 1957 or the Patents Act, 1970.]


OBJECTS AND REASONS
Clause 80.This clause provides that the provisions of the Act shall have overriding
effect over the other law.
