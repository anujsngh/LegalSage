CHAPTER 11
OFFENCES

66. Computer related offences

If any person, dishonestly or fraudulently, does any act referred to in section 43, he
shall be punishable with imprisonment for a term which may extend to three years or
with fine which may extend to five lakh rupees or with both.
Explanation.For the purposes of this section,
(a) the word dishonestly shall have the meaning assigned to it in section 24 of the
Indian Penal Code;
(b) the word fraudulently shall have the meaning assigned to it in section 25 of the
Indian Penal Code.
