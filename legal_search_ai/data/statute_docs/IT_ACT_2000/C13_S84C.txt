CHAPTER 13
MISCELLANEOUS

84C. Punishment for attempt to commit offences

Whoever attempts to commit an offence punishable by this Act or causes such an
offence to be committed, and in such an attempt does any act towards the commission of
the offence, shall, where no express provision is made for the punishment of such
attempt, be punished with imprisonment of any description provided for the offence, for a
term which may extend to one-half of the longest term of imprisonment provided for that
offence, or with such fine as is provided for the offence, or with both.
