THE FIRST SCHEDULE

[See sub-section (4) of section 1]
DOCUMENTS OR TRANSACTIONS TO WHICH THE ACT SHALL NOT APPLY

Description of documents or transactions
1. A negotiable instrument (other than a cheque) as defined in section 13 of the
Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881.
2. A power-of-attorney as defined in section 1A of the Powers-of-Attorney Act, 1882.
3. A trust as defined in section 3 of the Indian Trusts Act, 1882.
4. A will as defined in clause (h) of section 2 of the Indian Succession Act, 1925,
including any other testamentary disposition by whatever name called.
5. Any contract for the sale or conveyance of immovable property or any interest in
such property.]
