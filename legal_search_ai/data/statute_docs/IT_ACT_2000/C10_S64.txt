CHAPTER 10
THE [[ X X X ] APPELLATE TRIBUNAL]

64. Recovery of [penalty or compensation]

A [penalty imposed or compensation awarded] under this Act, if it is not paid, shall
be recovered as an arrear of land revenue and the licence or theb
[Electronic Signature]
Certificate, as the case may be, shall be suspended till the penalty is paid.

OBJECTS AND REASONS
Clause 64.This clause provides for recovery of penalty as an arrear of land revenue
and for suspension of the licence or Digital Signature Certificate till the penalty is paid.
